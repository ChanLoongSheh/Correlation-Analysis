Notes:
- E_scores are called 'scores' in PCA because they are projections of samples (here, λ) onto components;
  in this application, they are the eigensensitivity spectra E(λ).
- sqrt(explained_variance) is the standard deviation of E(λ) along each component;
  scikit-learn's singular_values satisfy σ_j = sqrt((N_λ - 1) * explained_variance_j).
- All CSVs include headers and wavelength λ [µm] as the row index where applicable.
